Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Who can use Campus Cycle? ▶
A: Any student or staff member of the campus can use Campus Cycle to buy, sell, exchange, or donate items.
Q: How do I add a product? ▶
A: Click on "Add Your Product" in the navigation bar and fill out the form with your product details.
Q: Is there any fee for listing items? ▶
A: No, listing items on Campus Cycle is completely free.
Q: How do I contact the seller? ▶
A: You can view the seller's contact details on the product page after logging in.
Q: Can I donate items? ▶
A: Yes, you can mark your item as a donation while adding a new product.
